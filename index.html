About

WEM Marine is an offshore support vessel company operating a fleet of state-of-the-art vessels to the offshore wind industry. We are committed to building strong long-term relationships with our clients through exceptional customer service.

Operating only class approved vessels with ISM we continually look to improve efficiency, safety and value for our clients.

Our offshore support vessels are suitable during any phase of an offshore project; Planning, Survey, Construction, Diving operations, Cable-laying, Testing, Commissioning and Maintenance.
